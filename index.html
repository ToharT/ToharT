FAQ

How soon do I get my order?

You will receive the products you ordered within 1 to 2 weeks. If you want to shorten the waiting time you can order by express delivery for an additional 50 NIS.

If I'm not happy with the items I got?

You can return what you bought and get the money back or switch to another product.

Would I be able to order some furniture in a different color?

Yes. At the end of your order, you can add color to a specific item you ordered

Would I be able to pick up my own order instead of receiving it by delivery?

Yes. You can pick her up from the branch nearest your house.
